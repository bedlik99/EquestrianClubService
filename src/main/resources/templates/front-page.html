Our offers include


All riding levels lessons

The lunge classes are intended for beginners who have their first contact with a horse, as well as for riders returning to classes after a long break. These lessons last half an hour. The horse is led by an instructor on a special rope, the so-called lunge. The rider is familiarized with the basic rules of horse riding, the horse's gaits, and the rules of proper seating.

Summer and winter camps

Horseback riding 2-3 hours a day Theoretical classes: horse riding and horse riding, preparation for the bronze exams and a silver PZJ equestrian badge, a friendly atmosphere, forest tours, bonfires, discos, shooting classes, great home-made food. The center is equipped with its own indoor arena. We are certified by the Polish Equestrian Federation.

Outdoor Events

Events for a companies - approximately 200 people. (Tents are included). Provide tent details at time of booking. All tent placements must be discussed with the Supervisor or Lead Hand prior to an event. A Development Permit is required for all heated tent structures as per the Fire Prevention Officer.
